LYTICS DOCUMENTATION SNIPPET
----------------------------
How to Build an Audience Segment in Lytics:

1. Access your Lytics dashboard at https://www.lytics.com/.
2. Click on “Audiences” in the navigation menu.
3. Choose “Create Audience” and define rules based on user behavior or profile data.
4. Review the estimated audience size and click “Save.”
5. Lytics will process your data to build the audience segment automatically.
