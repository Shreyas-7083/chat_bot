ZEOTAP DOCUMENTATION SNIPPET
----------------------------
How to Integrate Your Data with Zeotap:

1. Log in to Zeotap at https://docs.zeotap.com/.
2. Navigate to the “Integrations” section.
3. Select your data source type (e.g., CSV, Amazon S3).
4. Provide the required credentials or connection details.
5. Zeotap will begin ingesting your data for further processing and audience building.
